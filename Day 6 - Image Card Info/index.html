Thalapathy Vijay

Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema.

SEE MORE

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (b. June 22, 1974, Chennai), nicknamed Thalapathy, is a Tamil cinema icon known for films like Ghilli, Thuppakki, and Master. Renowned for his dance, action roles, and mass appeal, he balances acting, singing, and philanthropy. In 2024, he entered politics with Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam.
